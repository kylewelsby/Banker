If you've forgotten your card reader, don't worry. You can still:
    1.   Borrow a card reader from someone else
    2.   Use Telephone Banking*
    3.   Get Basic Access to Online Banking below
 
Providing you have a Connect/Electron debit card and have previously opted to use this service, Basic Access will allow you to check your account balance, check recent transactions, and transfer money between your Barclays online accounts.
 
If you have not already opted in to Basic Access then you will need to login using your PINsentry card reader, and go to 'Customise my Online Settings' and choose Manage Basic Access.
 
If you have lost your card reader, please call us on 0845 600 2323**.
 
NB Basic Access is not currently available to Business customers.
