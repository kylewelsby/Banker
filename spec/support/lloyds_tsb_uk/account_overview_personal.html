Help & Support

  • How do I stop receiving paper statements?

    To stop receiving paper statements, use the 'Go paperless' link  on your account statement page. Follow the instructions, then click to confirm.

  • How far back can I search for items on my account?

    You can view, print and download statements dating back to July 2002, free of charge. If you  have gone paperless, you can also order paper copies of your statement free of charge. If you still receive paper statements, you will incur a charge for ordering statements.

  • There’s a transaction on my statement that I don’t recognise. What should I do?

    If you have any queries about transactions appearing on your statement, please call us on 0845 3000 000. As a precaution, you should also log out of Internet Banking.

  • What accounts can I see online?

    As a personal customer, you can use Internet Banking to view most of your accounts. However, there are some you can’t see, such as Mortgage Accounts and Term Deposit Accounts.
