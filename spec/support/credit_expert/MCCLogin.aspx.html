Important security notice regarding email communication

Phishing is where criminals seek to obtain your private information by sending emails to thousands of people, claiming to be from organisations such as banks or credit reference agencies such as CreditExpert. If you receive an email or any other communication that looks suspicious and claims to have come from CreditExpert, please forward it to customerservice@creditexpert.co.uk then delete it.

Under no circumstances should you click on any link with in the email, reply to the email or provide any of the requested details.
